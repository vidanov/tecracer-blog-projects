Pricing Explanation

The monthly price is calculated as follows:

  1. Total runs per month = Number of Canaries × Runs per Hour × 24 hours × 30 days
  2. Subtract 100 free runs from the total
  3. Multiply the remaining runs by the price per run for the selected region
